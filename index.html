Our Mission

We strive to produce the highest quality horses for sport, work, and companionship alike. Every horse born on our farm has a purpose, and was bred with special attention to conformation, health, and temperament. Our goal is only to improve the horses of the Reef

Pallas Riding Horse

Our Pallas Riding Horses have continously reached high levels of dressage, show-jumping, cross-country, and have even qualified as War Horse grade stock.
Our lines are both home-bred, and from some of the best bloodlines that Pallas has to offer. Some of our most well-known alumni are VBF Red Giant, VBF Stars Over Mars, and VBF Beyond The Heliopause, all of whom have reached the very top level in their discipline, as well have contributed to producing some of the best War Horses in the Reef.
